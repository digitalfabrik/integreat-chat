The Zentrale Ausländerbehörde – ZAB Oberbayern
The ZAB Oberbayern is responsible for refugees in the reception centres, for asylum-seekers and for rejected asylum-seekers of different countries of origin. Its tasks include:

Decisions about Aufenthaltsgestattung (temporary residence permit), Duldung (temporary postponement of deportation), permits for training and employment
Establishing identity by questioning
Advice on returning and funding for your return
Ending stays

If the ZAB is responsible for you, you usually have a fixed contact person and a file number. Always include this file number in any letters you write to the ZAB.
