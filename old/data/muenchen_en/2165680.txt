Spielhaus am Westkreuz – Parent-child meeting point for children up to the age of 3 and their parents
The parent-child meeting point is a drop-in and free offer for 0 to 3 years with their parents. The centre has many areas: There is an open play area in which parents can try out exercises with their older children and a crawling carpet for babies. There are different crafts available at the creative table, with materials that the children can also use at home.
The teacher there is happy to answer questions on parenting, nutrition and health of children. A midwife and an employee from the early years support programme come once a month and are happy to advise and help you. Singing games are often useful for parents and children when learning German.
Who’s it for: Parents with children between the ages of 0 and 3 from the city districts of Aubing - Lochhausen - Langwied
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
