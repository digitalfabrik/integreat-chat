Gastro Akademie
The Gastro Akademie was launched in January 2021. It is a qualification programme for people with a migrant or refugee background and trains them specifically for the catering sector.
Who’s it for: People with a refugee or migrant background and people with migration-related support needs
Access requirements: from 18 years of age with (ideally) language level B1, at least A2
The service is free, any travel costs incurred must be covered by the participants.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
