Advice centres for parents, children, adolescents and families
Advice in issues of family life, difficulties at school and parenting issues, if you have worries about children or adolescents, in the event of separation and divorce, in issues concerning access and custody laws.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Parents, mothers, fathers, children, adolescents and other family members
 Interpreters can be requested if necessary.
You can bring your children with you.
The service is free.
Which parenting advice centre is responsible for you depends on your  address: enter your address, click on the field “Weiter” (“continue”) and the parenting advice centre that you can contact will be displayed.
