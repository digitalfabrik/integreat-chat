
Network from and for women and girls in Bavaria with a disability
The Netzwerkfrauen Bayern is an open association. Every girl or woman with a disability can be part of the network and everyone who thinks our topics are important can join in to help! We exchange experiences and information about all aspects of life: Job and education, family and partnership, medical issues, political issues. We speak about dealing with the authorities and laws but also about hobbies and leisure time.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Women and girls with a disability or chronic illness
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
