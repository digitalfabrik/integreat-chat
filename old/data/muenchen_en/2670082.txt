Addiction counselling service of the city of Munich
With the addiction counselling service, you can get help for problems with legal and illegal drugs, and with gambling. The offer is for people who are affected themselves and for relatives of those who are addicted.The advice is confidential.
 Find out more
 Interpreters are available on request.
You can bring your children with you.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free of charge.
