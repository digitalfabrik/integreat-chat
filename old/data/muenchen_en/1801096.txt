General information - find work
The work administration institutions are primarily responsible for finding work for you. These are the »» Agentur für Arbeit (Employment Agency) and the »» Jobcenter. Independent of your residence status or the duration of your unemployment, one of these two contact points will be responsible for you. Both have a large pool of vacant jobs and apprenticeships. As well as finding you work, they also have other possibilities to support you (for example occupational qualification)
There are various additional advice and support centres, some of which are financed with public money. There it’s usually easy to get an appointment quickly and the access requirements can be lower.
