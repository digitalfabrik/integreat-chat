JIZ – school stress counselling
Are you experiencing stress at school? Do you sometimes not want to go there any more? Or are you a parent who notices how much school stresses their child? In the JIZ, psychological specialists from the PIBS (psychological information and advice for pupils, parents and teachers) offer a regular consultation session on the topic of mobbing for school pupils and their parents.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: School pupils and their parents
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
Please make an appointment.
