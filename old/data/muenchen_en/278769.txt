Café Bellevue di Monaco meeting place
Our info café is organised and run together with refugees and non-refugees. Here, you can eat delicious, inexpensive food (tea and water are free), and also use the advice services, do an internship, join in as a volunteer, attend cultural and political events and simply meet other people.

All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
