Citizens from the EU and EEA
People from the following countries of the European Union (EU) or the  European Economic Area (EEA) enjoy so-called “freedom of movement”:
Belgium, Bulgaria, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Greece, Great Britain, Ireland, Iceland, Italy, Croatia, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, The Netherlands, Norway, Austria, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Sweden, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, The Czech Republic, Hungary and Cyprus
You and your family members from third-countries do not need a work permit. If you would like to work as a self-employed person, please get in touch with the  Gewerbeamt in the Kreisverwaltungsreferat (district administration office).
