Berufsschule
You receive initial vocational training at a Berufsschule or Berufsfachschule (vocational college). The training can be either in the »» dual system (company-based training and attending the Berufsschule) or as a solely »» college-based training (Berufsfachschulen).
The vocational colleges have different deadlines and registration requirements. There are information evenings that take place at the Berufsfachschulen every year before registration. You can find our information about how to register by contacting each college directly.
