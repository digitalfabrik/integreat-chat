

Assistance for subsistence
Who for: For people who are in the asylum procedure or have "Duldung" status.
If you do not earn enough money, you will receive assistance to live. This is stated in the Asylum Seekers' Benefits Act (AsylbLG).
The support relates to subsistence: you get food, accommodation, clothing, products for health and personal care and household items. In addition, you receive a monthly amount of money for personal needs.
In case of illness (sickness certificate), pregnancy and childbirth, you will receive extra support.
The amount of money and the value of the benefits in kind depend on the  Regelbedarfsstufen (standard needs levels).
Assistance in case of illness
If you are ill, you will get a sickness certificate from the Office for Housing and Migration. You can choose your own doctor's office.
