Advice centre for girls and young women – IMMA e. V.
The IMMA advice centre helps girls and young women with all their questions and in different problem situations focussing on violence.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Girls and young women up to 27 years of age
 Interpreters are available but must be requested in advance.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
Please make an appointment. We currently offer appointments in person, by telephone or via video consultation.
