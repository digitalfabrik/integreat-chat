Jugendinformationszentrum (JIZ) – Information on the Munich holiday programmes
The Munich holiday portal  www.ferien-muenchen.de is organised by the JIZ and gives information about municipal, federal and publicly funded holiday offers for children and adolescents in Munich. In the JIZ there are also many leaflets and flyers on leisure activities for children and adolescents, particularly in the school holidays. The info material is free; there is also a PC available for internet research.
Who’s it for: adolescents and young adults from 14 to 26 years and all those who are involved with young people (parents, teachers, youth work specialists)
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
