Conversation group – have courage to speak German
People with a migration background and German language level at A2/B1 level are warmly welcome to join our conversation group. We discuss situations from everyday life such as school and work as well as about topics that are important for participants. The aim is to make you feel more confident when forming sentences and speaking.
 Interpreters are available.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
Places are limited.
Please make an appointment by telephone.
