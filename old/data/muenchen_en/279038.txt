Afternoon get-together in the Generation Garden
At the afternoon get-together you can get in touch with people from the surrounding area, be creative, stay for a while and discuss things. You can also get support in social matters.
Who’s it for:  Children and adolescents, adults, families in München North, above all in Milbertshofen
 Interpreters are available for the languages Turkish and English.
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
