
Online videos on the asylum procedure
The Bavarian Refugee Council and the Munich Refugee Council have developed videos that explain the asylum procedure. They are a guide and support refugees in being able to navigate their asylum procedure in an informed and independent manner.
The videos are available in different languages.
The videos are not a substitute for personal and detailed counselling. They are a basis and provide information on how to avoid mistakes and omissions.
