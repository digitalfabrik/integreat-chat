Bewegt – exercise for women
This low-threshold drop-in offer takes place in a room which cannot be looked into from outside. The multilingual group leaders have a migration background. The offer includes one hour of exercises for women followed by 30 minutes of exercises for children.
Who’s it for: Mothers and children with a migration background
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free, however, we do strongly request a donation.
