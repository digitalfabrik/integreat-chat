
Barrier-free (public) toilets in Munich
On the website  inclus you can find an overview of accessible toilets in Munich.
If you click on the location symbol, you can see your own location on the map and where the nearest toilet is: 
If you click on a toilet, you will get more detailed information about its facilities:- floor plan- Handles installed- door width- slope of the ramp- use with or without Euro-toilet key
 German, English
You can also give a rating about the toilet.
The service is free of charge.
