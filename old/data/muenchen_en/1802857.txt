Cycling courses for female migrants
Do you want to learn how to ride a bike? Come to our cycling courses.  »» Donna Mobile offers cycling courses for female migrants in partnership with the Sportamt (Sports Office) of the city of Munich. There are courses for beginners and for advanced cyclists. It’s also possible to go on cycling trips with the (female) course leaders. The courses take place from April to October.
Donna Mobile also offers you the possibility to train as a cycling trainer yourself.
Who’s it for: Women with a migration background, with or without experience in riding a bicycle
Places are limited.
The service costs: Basic course 50 EUR, advanced course 30 EUR, trip 20 EUR, training to be a bicycle course teacher in four modules, 50 EUR
 Our course teachers are multilingual. The courses can be offered in the following languages: German, Turkish, Russian, Croatian, English
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
