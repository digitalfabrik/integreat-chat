Minimum wage
In Germany, there is a legal minimum wage. The current minimum wage is 12 EUR per hour. This amount is raised on a regular basis.
You are also not allowed to be paid less than the minimum wage by being forced to work longer hours than contractually agreed.
The minimum wage does not apply to:

Adolescents under the age of 18 without a completed job qualification
Apprentices
The long-term unemployed during the first six months of their employment after ending their unemployment
Interns in certain forms of internship (school or study-related internship or for the purposes of job orientation for up to three months)
Voluntary activities
