
Advice centre Emergency number for women Munich
We support women* who have experienced sexual harassment at work and or in public, have suffered rape and or sexual abuse and have been affected by stalking and/or digital violence. We also advise relatives and professional caregivers.
Women* includes all persons who identify as women.
Counselling services:

We are at your side in crisis situations.
We support you in dealing with the effects of violence.
We answer your questions.
We explain your rights to you.
We develop solutions with you so that you are able to recover.
What you tell us won’t be shared with anyone else.
We have a duty of confidentiality.
You can stay anonymous and don’t need to give your name
Our services: Telephone-based counselling, online counselling, video counselling, crisis line
Personal counselling, walk and talk and trauma therapy

Group offers:
WEN DO Self-assertion and self-defence, therapy and resource groups, trauma-sensitive yoga
 Find out more
Who for: All women* who have been affected by sexualised violence, as well as their relatives and professional caregivers.
 Interpreters are available.
Childcare is available.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises, as well as a WC suitable for the disabled are available.  We continue to work on identifying and removing existing barriers. You need something else? Talk to us!
The counselling offer is free; there are minimal fees for group sessions and courses.
Please make an appointment.
