KIBS – Contact, information advice centre for boys* and young men* who are affected by sexual violence and/or violence at home

Personal advice given by a choice of female or male specialists
Telephone advice with suppressed telephone number display
Trauma-focused and family-focused help
Group offer for boys who have experienced violence at home

 Find out more
Who’s it for: Boys and young men up to the age of 27 who are affected by sexual violence; boys from the age of six who have experienced sexual violence,
Parents, families, friends, other affected persons, pedagogic specialists
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
