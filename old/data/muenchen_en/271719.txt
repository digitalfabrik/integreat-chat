Health advice centre on sexually transmitted infections (STIs) including AIDS
Anonymous and confidential advice and testing possibilities for all sexually transmitted infections (STIs) including HIV.
The gynaecological counselling session on Wednesday is focussed on women who find it difficult to access medical care.
 www.muenchen.de/aidsberatung
Who’s it for: Adolescents and adults of all ages
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
