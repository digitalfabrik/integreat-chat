Fachstelle Asylrecht des Münchner Flüchtlingsrats
The Fachstelle Asylrecht des Münchner Flüchtlingsrats (Office for Asylum Rights of the Munich Council of Refugees), serves as a contact point for full-time officials, volunteers and refugees for all questions regarding asylum and residence rights. With regular training programmes and seminar days, refugees and supporters are trained in relevant legal issues and developments.
In addition, refugees and volunteers are able to get personal advice free in English or German.
Who’s it for: Volunteers, refugees and full-time officials
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
Apart from the training programmes for full-time officials, the programme is free.
