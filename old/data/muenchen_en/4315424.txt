
Emergency medical services
Ambulance in the event of danger to life
 112
You are only allowed to dial the emergency number if there is a real emergency. If you misuse the service, you can be sentenced to up to two years in prison according to § 145 par. 1 fig. 1 German Criminal Code (StGB).

On-call medical service
 116117 
Free of charge hotline available throughout Germany for non-life threatening illnesses, outpatient medical help outside the opening times of doctors’ practices, referral to the next available on-call medical service.
