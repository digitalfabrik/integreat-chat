The Verein zur Betreuung und Integration behinderter Kinder und Jugendlicher (Supervision and integration of disabled children and adolescents or BiB) e. V. offers advice about these and many other questions about the topic of disability. We are happy to give you general information on the telephone, but will also advise you individually in a personal conversation with us in our office. The advice from BiB. e. V. is confidential and free. We take time for you and are ready to listen!
