Help for female offenders and released prisoners
Have you received a fine? You can’t pay? Do you have to do community work as a condition of parole? Do the authorities want to arrest you? Do you have to go to prison soon?
Please contact us. We provide counselling if you have to pay a fine, are on probation, need to do community work as well as before and after you have to go to prison.
The sooner you come to us, the better we can help you! We offer counselling to women by women in a safe environment.
The Sozialdienst katholischer Frauen München e.V. (SkF) is a women’s association in the Catholic Church. The SkF provides counselling and care whatever your religion or nationality.
Who’s it for: Women from 21 years of age who have been sentenced to pay a fine or have to do legally ordered work requirements.
Counselling
-     before the prison sentence
-     in the JVA München and JVA Aichach (Munich and Aichach prisons)
-     as preparation for release
-     Help after release
 Interpreters are available for the languages English, Bulgarian and Russian.
You can bring your children with you.
Barrier-free access to buildings and rooms as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
