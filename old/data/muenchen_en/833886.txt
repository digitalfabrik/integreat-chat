AbilityAid
Open counselling service for people with a refugee or migration background with disabilities and/or chronic illnesses, including computer courses (e.g. Word, Excel, Internet) and German-language practice courses.
Who is it for: People with disabilities and/or chronic illness, relatives.
The service is free.
 Interpreters are available by arrangement.
You can bring your children with you.
Barrier-free access to the buildings and premises is available.
