KulturBunt Neuperlach
We offer our audience a colourful, diverse and intercultural cultural programme for all cultures and ages. We promote the performing and visual arts, create free space for exhibitions and stages for theatre and music professionals.
Who’s it for: all ages and cultures
The prices for our events vary, some are free.
You can bring your children with you.
All buildings and rooms of the cultural centre have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
