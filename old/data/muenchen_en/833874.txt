Leisure facilities of the KJR
There are a total of 50 leisure facilities run by the Kreisjugendring in Munich. Here, children and young people can spend time with their peers. They can do what they enjoy: try out table tennis, table football, billiards and other games, listen to music and make music, do handicrafts, sports, dance, go on the internet ... or just talk and laugh with others. The KJR is there for all children and young people.
One of these centres is bound to be near you!
Who’s it for: Children, adolescents, young adults
All the services are free.
