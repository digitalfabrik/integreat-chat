What are BildungsLokale?
BildungsLokale are places where you can get information, advice and expert interaction on the topic of education. The programme is designed for all citizens and education stakeholders in the city district.
The teams of the BildungsLokale offer counselling and are also active out-and-about in the district. They see themselves as important guides for educational issues at any age.
