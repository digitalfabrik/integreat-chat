Familienpass
The Familienpass (family pass) is available to all Munich families and families from the districts of Dachau, Ebersberg, Freising, München and Starnberg. It is valid for an entire calendar year and contains numerous exclusive workshop and leisure programmes, vouchers (for example twice free entry into one of the city swimming pools) and many attractive reductions.
The Familienpass (family pass) is valid for two adults and max. four children up to the age of 17, and costs 6 EUR as a regular price. Families with more than four children can receive one further, additional Familienpass (only available from the  Stadtjugendamt at Elisenhof, Luitpoldstraße 3, room 3002/Infothek).
Needy families can also receive the Familienpass free.
Eligibility requirements:

Social need / social-pedagogic supervision
Low income (the income limit is checked individually)
Participation in federal voluntary service, voluntary social  or ecological year (FSJ etc.)
Receiving one of the following benefits:
- Help with living costs or Grundsicherung (subsistence) (SGB XII)
- Arbeitslosengeld II (unemployment benefit II) (SGB II)
- Benefits under the Asylbewerberleistungsgesetz or AsylbLG (Benefits for Asylum Seekers Act)
- Wohngeld (housing allowance) or Kinderzuschlag (child benefit supplement)

Documents required:
Approval letter regarding proof of income, assets and rent OR report from the supervising pedagogic specialist service
