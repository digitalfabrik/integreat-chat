Psychological service for foreigners
Psychological support for migrants, newly immigrated persons, EU citizens and refugees. Supervised independent living for psychologically ill people with a migration and refugee background in the Munich area.
 Find out more
 The consultation takes place in these languages: German, Serbian, Croatian, Bosnian, Italian, Greek, Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, Turkish, Romanian, Bulgarian, English, French, Dari. Interpreters are available for all other languages.
You can bring your children with you.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
