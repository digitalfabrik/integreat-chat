
Chamber of Industry and Commerce (IHK) for Munich and Upper Bavaria
The IHK is an organisation with various tasks relating to training.
For example, it is responsible for the registration of training contracts. It controls the quality of vocational training and organises the final examinations. There are also offers for vocational orientation, fairs and events.
