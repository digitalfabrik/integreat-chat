Special price for school beginners
Pupils who are starting school in Munich for the first time or changing to a secondary school (Mittelschule, Realschule, Gymnasium or Gesamtschule), can receive a one-off special payment for purchasing equipment like a school satchel (Schulranzen), pencil case, paintbox etc.
Who’s it for:
Pupils,

who have the München-Pass and /or
whose family are receiving one of the following benefits:
- Help with living costs or Grundsicherung (subsistence) (SGB XII)
- Arbeitslosengeld II (unemployment benefit II) (SGB II)
- Benefits under the Asylbewerberleistungsgesetz or AsylbLG (Benefits for Asylum Seekers Act)
- Wohngeld (housing allowance) or Kinderzuschlag (child benefit supplement)

Documents required:

Letter of approval OR information letter from the Sozialbürgerhaus or the Amt für Wohnen und Migration (Office for Housing and Migration)
Confirmation of the school that your child is starting school for the first time or moving to a secondary school

You make the application in your local Sozialbürgerhaus. or at the Amt für Wohnen und Migration if you receive benefits according to the asylum-seekers benefits law.
You receive the special payment in cash at the cashier’s office of your local Sozialbürgerhaus or the Amt für Wohnen und Migration (Office for Housing and Migration).
Important: You can receive the payment up until 31 October of the current year.
