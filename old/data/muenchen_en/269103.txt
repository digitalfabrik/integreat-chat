Agentur für Arbeit München
The Agentur für Arbeit München (Munich Employment Agency) offers new immigrants and refugees a contact point for questions about work, training and career.
We support you individually and – if necessary– step-by-step with your integration in training and work. You can get job skills and job-related German knowledge in various programmes geared towards the job market. The long-term aim is to gain skilled workers for the Munich job market.
Long-term or periodic support from the Agentur für Arbeit (Employment Agency) offers you the chance of career development to become a skilled employee.
 Find out more
Who’s it for:
• Asylum seekers with Aufenthaltsgestattung (temporary residence permit) or Duldung (temporary postponement of deportation) from countries with a good possibility of being able to remain in Germany, aged between 15 and 65 years
• People with recognised refugee status and residence permit (with entitlement to Arbeitslosengeld I - unemployment benefit I) aged between 15 and 65 years
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
