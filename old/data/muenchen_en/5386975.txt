
School-based vocational training
Full-time school-based vocational education and training
There are two types of training in Germany:
Dual system ("" dual training).Pupils learn both at a vocational school and in a training company.
School-based training at a vocational schoolThe students only attend school and learn everything there.
The school-based training usually lasts 1 - 3 years. Depending on the school, there are different types of training and admission requirements. You can get detailed information directly from the school.
School-based training is usually found in professions in the fields of technology, health and social services, art and foreign languages.
