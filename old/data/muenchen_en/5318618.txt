
Ausländerbehörde München (Munich Immigration Office) in the District Administration Service (KVR)
Tasks:
The Ausländerbehörde München (Munich Immigration Office) regulates the residency of foreign persons in Munich.
Dates:
Some services can be availed of from your home. However, for other services, it’s necessary for you to come to the Ausländerbehörde (Immigration Office) in person. You need to make an appointment for this.
More detailed information can be found in our online service.
You can also use the contact form to tell us your concern. If necessary, you will be given an appointment for a personal interview once we have checked your application. Please find out in advance which documents you require.
