
JUNGS - Leisure group for gay and bisexual boys
The JUNGS are a leisure group for gay, bisexual and insecure boys between the ages of 18 and 27. If you would like to do something together and meet new people you are very welcome.
If you are unsure before your first participation or have questions about the group, you can contact us at any time.
Our meetings are a safe space. Therefore, no photos or videos will be taken. The offer is open to trans men and non-binary people.
 Learn more
The offer costs a contribution towards expenses for some activities.
