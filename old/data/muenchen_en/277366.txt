
Self-help group “Positive Women”
Women with HIV – we live positively!Leave loneliness and isolation behind – start meeting people, having discussion, do activities!In our self-help group we offer discussions about many issues, information, tips that are not written in any book, joint leisure activities and mutual support. We warmly invite you to get to know us!We meet once a month on Mondays from 18:30 – 20:30 (please call us for the exact date).
Who’s it for: Women living with an HIV infection / HIV-positive women
 Interpreters for English are available.
The service is free.
