Bellevue di Monaco - asylum counselling
Do you have a problem with your asylum and right of residence? Our asylum counselling team takes time to find a solution or refers you to a suitable advice service. Please bring your documents with you and someone to translate for you if necessary. We look forward to seeing you!
 Find out more
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
