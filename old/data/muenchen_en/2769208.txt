FamAra – Migration advice for homeless families
Advice centre for families without their own accommodation / without shelter. Clarification of the housing situation, childcare services for children and adolescents and, if necessary, counselling about returning to the home country. Referrals for sleeping places in the Kälteschutz or Übernachtungsschulz cold protection programme or homelessness programmes. Visiting social work (street work) if homeless families with children are registered. Setting up post addresses.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Homeless families with European nationality or a European residence status. Families with children who stay overnight in homeless shelters open throughout the year. Homeless pregnant women from the seventh month of pregnancy. Parents or guardians who are threatened by homelessness or who are affected by precarious housing situations.
 Interpreters are available for the following languages: English, Spanish, Bulgarian, Rumanian
You can bring your children with you.
