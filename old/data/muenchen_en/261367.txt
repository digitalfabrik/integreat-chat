rosa Alter
Advice for lesbian, gay, trans*identity and inter*sexual seniors on social, socio-legal and psycho-social issues. Referrals to voluntary helpers. Networking in the context of individual counselling, development of an individual care network. Networking with the LGBTI* organisations, associations and groups as well as with the conventional care for seniors. Advanced training on the issues of LGBTI* and aging.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: LGBTI* from the age of around 50 and their families
