
Municipal complaints office for problems in care for the elderly
When older people are in care facilities (inpatient) or are being cared for at home (outpatient), questions or problems can arise. We take care of your concerns about this topic. You can also contact us if you have complaints about support in living (assisted living) or other forms of housing. 
Together with you, we will find a solution.
 Learn more
For whom:Senior citizensRelatives and caregiversLegal guardians and carersNeighboursAcquaintancesCarers
 Interpreters are available, please let us know in advance if required.
Children can be brought along.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises as well as a toilet suitable for the disabled are available.
The service is free of charge.
