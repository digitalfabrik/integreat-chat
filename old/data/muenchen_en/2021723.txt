
Grundschule
Registration for Grundschule:
Children who will be six by 30 June are required to go to school. The parents have to register them at a public Grundschule, the  Sprengelschule.
You can find  information about registration here.
Following consultation with and a recommendation from the school, parents of children who will turn six between 1 July and 30 September can decide whether their child will start school in the current school year or the next one. The Sprengelgrundschulen can provide you with further information.

If you have questions about registering for Grundschule, you can also contact the municipal Referat für Bildung und Sport: 089/23396779
After leaving Grundschule:
All children attend Grundschule up to year 4. Here they learn to read and write and study maths. Towards the end of year 4 (in May) they receive a so-called ‘Übertrittszeugnis’ (transfer certificate). In Bavaria, the marks on this certificate determine which school the child will attend: a »» Mittelschule (years 5 to 9 or 10)a »» Realschule (years 5 to 10) or a »» Gymnasium (years 5 to 12/13). The three types of school differ according to the subjects they teach and the level of the lessons.
There are also two special types of school:the »» Orientierungsstufe (years 5 and 6, followed by transfer to Mittelschule, Realschule or Gymnasium from year 7) and the »» Gesamtschule (years 5 to 7, from year 8 three different leaving qualifications are possible: Mittelschulabschluss, Realschulabschluss, gymnasiale Oberstufenreife der naturwissenschaftlich-technologischen Ausbildungsrichtung).
