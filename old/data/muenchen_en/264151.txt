Hospital emergency departments
The emergency departments of the hospitals, which are open 24 hours a day, are reserved for serious and life-threatening illnesses (such as stroke, heart attack, accident injury, head injury, poisoning ...). In all other cases, doctors who work in a medical practice or on-call medical services are responsible.
