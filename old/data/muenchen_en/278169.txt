refugees@diversity – buddy programme
The buddy programme for refugees, refugees@diversity, provides you with a buddy at your side who accompanies you in your first visits to our leisure groups, thereby making it easier for you to access our community.
Who’s it for: LGBTIQ* People who had to flee their home country between the ages of 14 and 27
There is a small charge for some activities.
Please make an appointment.
