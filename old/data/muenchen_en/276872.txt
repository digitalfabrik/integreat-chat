Donna Mobile AKA e. V.
Donna Mobile offers counselling for female migrants on different topics in their native language. Various preventative health services and exercise classes take place regularly. Our educational programme includes three qualification courses.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Female migrants as well as their families
There are minimal costs for the services.
Our employees are all migrants themselves or have a migration background. This means we can offer our services in different languages.
