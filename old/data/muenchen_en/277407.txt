Specialist outpatient clinic for eating disorders and muscle dysmorphia
We provide advice in all problems regarding eating disorders and weight. Our advice is available to children, adolescents, women and men:

who suffer from their eating behaviour
who already know that they have an eating disorder
who are indirectly affected as parents, teachers, friends, partners, colleagues, superiors, carers... by a person’s eating disorder

What is an eating disorder? You can find more information  here.
As well as counselling, we also offer treatment for anorexia, bulimia, obesity, muscle dysmorphia.
  Find out more
 Interpreters are available.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
