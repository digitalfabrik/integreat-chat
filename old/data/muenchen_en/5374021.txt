
Web platform welcome.munich.de
The city of Munich presents itself as a business location at  welcome.munich. de. You can find information that can help you find your way around. And you can find offers from the city, the state of Bavaria or the Federal Republic of Germany. The topics are work and business, science, international communities, language and health.
 The information is in English.
Who for: For foreign professionals or new immigrants and their families.
