
Visitor visa (Schengen visa)
People who possess a valid Schengen visa (text on the visa label: “valid for Schengen states” in the respective language of the issuing country) can stay in the entire Schengen area for a period of 180 days to 90 days.
 Find out more (English)
Please note that you have to leave the countries within the validation period of your visa. You are required to leave the entire Schengen area.
A Schengen visa that has not been issued by the embassy for three months, can be extended in Germany. However, the conditions must still be met.
You are not allowed to change the purpose of the visa. The application must be justified, in particular

Force majeure,
Humanitarian, professional or serious personal reasons

can be taken into account.
If you do not have your visa extended by the »» Ausländerbehörde (Immigration Office) and leave only after the visa has expired, you are breaking the law. The Ausländerbehörde (Immigration Office) then checks to see if your stay must be ended. It would only be possible for you to re-enter the country if you were not subject to a re-entry ban. Legal action could also be taken against you.
