German conversation meet-up in the Seidlvilla
Twice a week, we talk about everyday life and learn new things about other cultures and people. In this way, we get to know new people, share experiences and improve our German knowledge. Registration is not required.
Who’s it for: Adolescents and adults
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
