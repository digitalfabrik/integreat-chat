Handwerkskammer
The Handwerkskammer München und Oberbayern has several tasks.

It is responsible for training in over 130 craft and trade professions.
It is the institute responsible for recognising job qualifications that were obtained abroad and correspond to a craft or trade here. You can find more information here »» about recognition of foreign job qualifications.
The Handwerkskammer also advises people who want to become self-employed craftspeople.

For young people with a migration background, the »» training agency offers an interesting service.
