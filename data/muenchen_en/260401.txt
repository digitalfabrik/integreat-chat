Advice in hostels for refugees
For persons with undecided asylum procedures and tolerated persons, there are advice centres in all hostels for refugees. The services of the refugee and integration advice service (FIB) includes the following content:

Information about rights and obligations during official procedures
Subsistence
Asylum procedure
Participation in society
Finding your way around the city
Health
Learning German
Education and work
Mediation in conflict situations
Encouraging peaceful coexistence
Organising voluntary support

If you need help and support, just ask the employees at the advice centre in your hostel.
