
Central contact point at the main railway station:
Arrivals will receive initial information here in their mother tongue (information on registering, referral to psychosocial (initial) counselling centres, onward journeys, information on accommodation etc.) Munich main railway station, Bahnhofsmission, platform 11 daily, from 00:00 to 24:00 089/594576 muenchen@bahnhofsmission.de More information
Registration in the arrival center for refugees from Ukraine:
It is important that you register with the Regierung von Oberbayern within the first 90 days of your stay in Munich so that you will be entitled to support if necessary.Registration takes place at the arrival centre. Arrival centre for Ukrainian refugees, Dachauerstraße 122, 80637 Munich
If you are registered and need accommodation, send us an email: ukraine-Verlegung.soz@muenchen.de
