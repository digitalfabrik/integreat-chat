Weltkinder (World Children) - readings in many languages
In various Munich city libraries, readings for children take place in the afternoons; not just in German but in many other languages too. You can find the location, dates and languages of the events here:   http://www.muenchner-stadtbibliothek.de/veranstaltungen
At the rubric “Veranstaltungssuche” enter “Weltkinder”.
Who’s it for: Children between the ages of 3 and 6
Most premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
