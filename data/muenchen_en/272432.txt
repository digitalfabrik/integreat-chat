Psychological service of the Arbeiterwohlfahrt München (AWO)
We are a specialist service for psychological counselling and therapy, offering:

Psychological diagnosis
Clearing and referrals
Counselling in issues of parenting
Psychological counselling and therapy
Couples and family therapy
Crisis intervention
Group offers
Counselling in issues of contact with children when parents separate

 Find out more
Who’s it for: Children, adolescents and adults, couples and families with a migration background who speak the languages: Turkish, Serbian, Croatian and Bosnian who are resident in Munich. People who had to flee their home country, who have been living in Munich for at least three years and can speak German or English.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
Please make an appointment by telephone.
