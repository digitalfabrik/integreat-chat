Crises counselling and suicide prevention
“Die Arche” offers advice for people in life crises situations, suicidal thoughts or after an attempted suicide as well as for people who have lost a close friend or relative through suicide.
 Find out  more
Who’s it for: Adults and teenagers over the age of 14
 Interpreters can be organised if several days’ notice is given.
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The  service is free.
