Residence with a visa
If you enter Germany with a visa with which a longer stay is not intended, you must leave Germany within the validity period of the visa – i.e. at the latest on the last day of validity.
A Schengen visa that has not been issued for three months can be extended in Germany for a maximum period of three months within a deadline of six months, if the requirements still exist. You are not allowed to change the purpose of the visa. The application must be justified, in particular

Force majeure,
Humanitarian, professional or serious personal reasons

can be taken into account.
If you leave later, without asking permission of the »» Ausländerbehörde (Immigration Office), your stay would be without permission and an infringement of the law. The Ausländerbehörde (Immigration Office) then checks how your stay ended. It would only be possible for you to re-enter the country if you were not subject to a re-entry ban. Legal action could also be taken against you.
