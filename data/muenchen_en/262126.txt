Bellevue di Monaco - migration counselling
Do you have a question or a problem about living and working in Germany? Just come along to us! We support adults with a migration or refugee background with our advice and referrals to offers of help and explain the further procedure in order to find a suitable solution.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: adult migrants
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
