German course for primary school children
Children who are in their 1st or 2nd school years and still have problems with the German language receive intensive language development here.
Who’s it for: Children from the age of six whose parents receive social benefits.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
It costs 40 EUR per month (money for materials).
Registration is required.
