
BAMF-NAvI information platform
The BAMF-NavI is a digital platform that helps in the search for  German courses / integration courses. Here you can find answers to the following questions:- Where can I find an integration course near me?- Where can I find an immigration counselling centre in my city?- In which period does a course take place?- What are the lesson times / when is the break?- Where are there still free places?- Is the course location barrier-free?- Where can I find a course with childcare?
