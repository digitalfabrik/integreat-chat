Municipal rent counselling office
The municipal rent counselling office in the Amt für Wohnen und Migration (Office for Housing and Migration) offers free-of-charge advice on all questions about residential tenancy law. If you wish, we will support you in looking for a lawyer who specialises in tenancy law.We advise you on the following topics, among others:

Rent raise / rent price
Utility costs / heating costs
Security deposit
Terminating a contract
Cosmetic/decorative repairs
Conflicts between landlords and tenants
Rights and duties of tenants and landlords

Tenancy law counselling for young people - Jugendinformationszentrum (JIZ)
The JIZ offers legal advice on questions regarding tenancy law for young people up to the age of 26. The consultation takes place via email with an external specialist lawyer. Those seeking advice can simply send their questions and relevant documents (e.g. tenancy agreement) to  info@jiz-muenchen.de
You can ask the following questions, for example:

We want to form a WG, what do we need to be aware of?
How does it work with commission and deposit?
What should be in the rent contract, and what should not be in the rent contract?
Am I allowed to rent it out to someone else?
Do I have to renovate when moving out?
What can I do if my contract is terminated (Kündigung)?

Who’s it for: Young tenants up to the age of 26 (as main tenant, not as member of a household/family)
The service is free.
Enquiries by email to:  info@jiz-muenchen.de https://jiz-muenchen.de
