Open-air swimming pools
The Munich open-air swimming pools are open from mid-May until the end of the summer holidays in September.
They are all open until 18:00 daily; on hot days until 19:00 or 20:00. You can find out how long the pool is open by clicking  here, on the swimming pool you want.
