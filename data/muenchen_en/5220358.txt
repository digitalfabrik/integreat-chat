
Immigration Counselling Service for Adults
Did you only recently arrive in Germany?Do you need help finding your way around in Germany?Have you been here for a longer time but still feel uncertain and alienated?
Are you aged 27 or over?Then the right place for you to get advice is the Migrationsberatung (Immigration Counselling Service).
The Migrationsberatung für erwachsene Zuwanderer (Immigration Counselling Service for Adults / MBE) advises, informs and supports migrants in settling into their new environment.
Come to the Migrationsberatung with your questions about the following topics:• German language courses / integration courses• Finances• Contact to authorities• School / education• Work / job• Family• Health• Housing
 We provide advice in German and many other languages.
We keep your personal information private.
We support you whatever your origin, religion and nationality.
Who’s it for: Advice for migrants from the age of 27 upwards
The service is free.
