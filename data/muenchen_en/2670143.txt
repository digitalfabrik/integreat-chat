Städtische Berufsschule zur Berufsintegration
Language promotion is the central focus of our work at the Berufsschule zur Berufsintegration (vocational school for occupational integration). As well as attaining a German school qualification, the pupils are prepared for an apprenticeship or attending a higher educational school.In addition to general education subjects such as German (as a second language), mathematics, politics and society, etc., we also offer a wide variety of practical subjects.
Who is it for: New immigrants and refugees who are 15 to 25 years old.
The service is free of charge.
Interpreters are available in Dari, Croatian, English, French, Greek, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Serbian, Spanish, Turkish and Ukrainian.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a WC for the disabled.
