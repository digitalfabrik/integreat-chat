
At Café Pflege, you can get information and advice on all aspects of care: Employment, internships, voluntary social year and federal voluntary service, training, university studies or qualification.
Who’s it for: Anyone interested in employment, qualification training and university studies in the nursing profession as well as employment in hospitals, nursing homes and outpatient care. Find out more
The service is free.
You can bring your children with you.
You do not need to make an appointment in advance. The advice takes place according to the “first come, first served” principle.
