JAL – Advice for young people on finding work and apprenticeships
Support in making the transition from school to the world of work, in choosing a career, finding an apprenticeship or training place, support during your training and in starting a new training course or job, and if you become unemployed
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Adolescents and young adults between the ages of 14 and 27
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
