
All-day services at schools
There are several forms of all-day tuition at schools in Munich:
At a contractual all-day school tuition of the class is spread out across the whole day. Learning and practising alternates with sport, musical and artistic activities. The children eat lunch together. There is no homework.
In a flexible all-day school the sport, musical and artist activities take place after academic lessons have been completed. The groups are made up of children from different school years.
At a cooperative all-day school the Grundschule offers all-day supervision and supervision in the school holidays until 18:00. The children are supervised in cooperation with pedagogic personnel. They eat lunch together.The cooperative all-day school can be combined with all-day years and the classic morning lessons.
