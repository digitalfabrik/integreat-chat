Working with people with a disability and a migration background
We, Die Nachbarschaftshilfe – German and foreign families, help people with disabilities and chronic illnesses to find the right authorities, organisations or offices for their concerns. We organise small events with useful tips about life in Munich, health, cultural integration etc.
Who’s it for: People with a disability or chronic illness and their families
 Interpreters are available in the languages of Russian and Bulgarian.
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
