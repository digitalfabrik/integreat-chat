
Opportunity Residence Act (Chancen-Aufenthaltsrecht)
On 31 December 2022, the Opportunity Residence Act came into force. The aim is to help people who have already been living in Germany for many years acquire legitimate residence status.
Prerequisites:

People with tolerated status (Duldung), who have been living in Germany since at least 31 October 2017 (entered before 01 November 2017), can acquire an 18-month residence permit.PrerequisitesInformation in other languagesApplications for right of residence: If you fulfil the necessary prerequisites (securing living costs, identity clarification, knowledge of the German language) during the 18-month period, you can apply for a right of residence pursuant to section 25a AufenthG or section 25b AufenthG.
