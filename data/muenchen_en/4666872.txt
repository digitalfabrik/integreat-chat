
Support in the case of homelessness- Zentrale Wohnungslosenhilfe der Stadt München 
We offer accommodation:in a municipal emergency centre, a simple hotel or boarding house or a hostel for the homeless. The type of accommodation you will be assigned depends on your personal situation. If you fulfil certain conditions, we can refer you to a women’s shelter or a clearing house, if there is a place free. We also discuss with you how we can help you find suitable long-term accommodation.
We provide advice and support you:In the municipal emergency accommodation, guest houses and clearing houses, we support you in finding a suitable place to live. Our employees also advise you with many other problems such as conflict in the family and partnership, difficulties in raising children, life crises and problems in old age or during illness. Among other things, we refer you to specialists for help in raising children, if you are in debt and if you would like to volunteer. In the accommodation for families we offer homework help for children, leisure activities and counselling for parents by trained educators.
We give you financial help:If you fulfil the legal requirements, we give you financial help in Munich’s twelve Sozialbürgerhäusern in the form of Arbeitslosengeld II (Unemployment benefit II) according to SGB II (Social Welfare Book Two) or Grundsicherung (subsistence) according to SGB XII (Social Welfare Book Twelve).The Jobcenter Munich will support you so that find a path back into the world of work. The market and integration department of the Jobcenter Munich advises you and organises placements on an individual basis for people who are able to work.If you need more help, the employees will discuss this among the team, with your permission.

Who’s it for: This offer is aimed at people with stable residence status - no Duldung (temporary postponement of deportation) or Aufenthaltsgestattung (temporary residence permit).
