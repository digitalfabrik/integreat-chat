
Drop-in kids café in the Kinder- und Jugendkulturwerkstatt Pasinger Fabrik
The Kindercafé is a meeting point for children and families. 
There are games and handicrafts, a reading corner and theatre events for children from the age of 4. You can build something yourself in our carpentry workshop.
Who’s it for: Children from 6 years of age (exception: Theatre events) and families.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free, with the exception of theatre performances.(You can get free tickets here ).
The children's café is closed during the school holidays.
