Advice for lesbian refugees and bisexual women
LeTRa supports lesbian and bisexual women. Have you experienced trauma, do you need psychosocial advice and/or support in the asylum process? Then you’ve come to the right place. We advise you, it doesn’t matter if you are trans, inter*, cis or gender queer.
Who’s it for: The service is aimed at all people who regard themselves as lesbian or bisexual women.
 Interpreters in all languages are available on request. We speak English, French and German.
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
