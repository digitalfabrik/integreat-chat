
Migration advice for citizens from Romania and Bulgaria
In the project “Bildung statt Betteln” (“Training rather than begging”) you can use the following offers for integration:

Integration counselling
Free legal advice for those in need
Labora, a project for those looking for work
Private tutoring for school pupils
Women’s groups
Conversation course for women
Family project “Gemeinsam in München” (“Together in Munich”)

Who’s it for: EU citizens of Romanian and Bulgarian origin
 Mother tongue counselling in Bulgarian and Romanian
You can bring your children with you.
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
