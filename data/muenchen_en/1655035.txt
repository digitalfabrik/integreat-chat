forum homosexualität münchen e. V. - lesbians and gays in history and culture
The forum homosexualität münchen e.V. - lesbians and gays in history and culture has set itself the aim of promoting and collecting articles and insights into the everyday life, culture and history of lesbians and gays, and making this accessible.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: LGBTIQ* people and anyone who is interested in the topic.
You can bring your children with you.
The service is free.
