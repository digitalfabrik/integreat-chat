Investigative centre for victims of domestic violence
People affected by domestic violence can get advice and be examined free here. Evidence can be gathered and preserved; injuries are documented in such a way that they can be used by the courts.
Who’s it for: Victims of any form of domestic violence (adults, aged 18 and over)
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free as long as it is used as described above. Assignment that go beyond these, such as writing a written evaluation, toxicological analyses and similar are chargeable.
