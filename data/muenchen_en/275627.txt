Central school psychological service
School psychological counselling can help pupils and their guardians:

if there are problems at school
if you want to improve your learning and concentration abilities
if you want to learn how to cope with fears, worries or stress in school.

You are welcome to make an appointment with us. In a personal discussion we will find solutions and possibilities for change together.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: Pupils of municipal secondary schools from the age of ten and their guardians
 Interpreters can be requested, please let us know if needed.
You can bring your children with you.
The service is free.
