Binationale Familien und Partnerschaften iaf e. V. – advice centre
We offer general social, psychological and legal counselling, couple & family therapy as well as legal advice. In cases of separation and divorce, we give advice on the topics of contact and parental care. In our premises, accompanied contact takes place together with parental counselling. There are extra group offers fromtime to time.
Who’s it for: bicultural and intercultural couples and families;Single people who live in interculturalcontexts
 Interpreters can be organised. Our advisers also speak several languages.
You can bring your children with you; childcare is usually organised for group activities.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
Please make an appointment.
