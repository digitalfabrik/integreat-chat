
Advice centres for older people and their relatives
The advice centres inform, support and help you make decisions about the topics linked with growing older, for example services of the nursing care insurance, planning and financing of care at home or as an inpatient, living and care arrangements for older people, in cases of dementia or other diseases affecting older people, economic help, dealing with the authorities, disabilities, power of attorney, care and advance health care directive, also known as “living will”.
