Who’s it for: Residents of Munich with disabilities and their family members
 Do you need an interpreter for the appointment? Please let us know this when you arrange the appointment so that we can organise this. This is a free service for you.
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
On the  website of the voluntary commissioner for the disabled you can find answers to many questions about disability.
Please arrange appointments for personal consultations in advance.
