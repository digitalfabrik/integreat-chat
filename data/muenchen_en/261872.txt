Sozialverband VdK Bayern – Kreisverband München
With more than 2 million members worldwide, the VdK is the largest social association in Germany providing a strong lobby for pensioners, people with disabilities, chronic illnesses, people needing care and their family members, families, older workers and unemployed persons. The charitable organisation advises and represents its members in all issues of social justice, gets involved in socio-political topics and invites you regularly to social events.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
Membership costs 72 EUR per year. If legal steps are taken, further charges apply.
