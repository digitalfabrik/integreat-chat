Advice centre for girls and young women in different life situations
We provide advice on the following topics, among others: School, careers advice, education, cultural difference, problems at home, problems with your boyfriend or girlfriend, sexuality, coming-out, pregnancy.
We offer confidential individual advice sessions  and counselling in small groups of up to three people. Please make an appointment.
Who’s it for: Girls* and women* between the ages of 12 and 27
The service is free.
