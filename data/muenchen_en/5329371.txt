
Business school Wirtschaftsschule), technical secondary school (Fachoberschule), vocational secondary school (Berufsoberschule)
These are vocational schools. They combine school subjects and vocational subjects. School subjects are, for example, English, German, mathematics. Vocational subjects are, for example, electrical engineering, media, nutrition.
Which subjects are taught depends on the school's specialisation.
The schools have different requirements.
Business school (Wirtschaftsschule)For whom: For students from the sixth grade upwards.The focus is on economic subjects.Qualification: Intermediate school leaving certificate.With this qualification, you can start an apprenticeship or attend the Fachoberschule (FOS) . In some cases, the Mittlerer Schulabschluss shortens the duration of the training.
For the Berufsoberschule (BOS) you need- a Mittlerer Schulabschluss (intermediate school leaving certificate)- a completed apprenticeship or several years of work experience.
There are different specialisations at FOS and BOS. For example, there is technology, business or health. You can choose what suits you best.Qualification: Fachhochschulreife (Fachabitur). Graduation with a second foreign language: Allgemeine Hochschulreife (Abitur).
