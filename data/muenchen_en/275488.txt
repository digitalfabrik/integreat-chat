diversityCafé
The diversityCafé opens its doors every Wednesday from 19:00 and warmly welcomes all young LGTIBQ* people and their friends. You can get to know new people and have plenty of good chats over drinks and snacks.
If you feel uncertain before taking part for the first time or have any questions, you are welcome to contact us at any time.
Who’s it for: young LGBTIQ* people and their friends up to the age of 27
For some activities there is a small charge to cover costs.
