Condrobs e. V. - Addiction counselling Munich
Outpatient addiction advice (alcohol, medicines, tobacco and illegal drugs, gambling and computer addiction) at all ages. The team at the advice centres can refer you on to further services and supports you in clarifying the costs. If it is necessary, we can also visit you in your home.
We also hold information events for specialists.
Outpatient rehabilitation and care (recognised by all funding bodies) are included in our service, as well as assisted living (outpatient intensive care in cases of addiction problems, also in your own living environment).
 Our advisers speak several languages, please enquire in advance.
