Advice Lebenshilfe München
Lebenshilfe München is a self-help association for people with primarily mental disability. People with a disability, parents and family members can get help from us. The Lebenshilfe is also a contact point for parents who are expecting a child with a disability. The advice deals with legal, social and practical life details.
Who’s it for: People with a disability and their family members
You can bring your children with you.
All premises have barrier-free access as well as a disabled toilet.
The service is free.
