JIZ – counselling for pupils and their parents affected by bullying
Psychological specialists from the PIBS (Psychologische Information und Beratung für Schülerinnen und Schüler, Eltern und Lehrkräfte [psychological information and advice for pupils, parents and teachers]) offer a regular consultation session on the topic of mobbing for school pupils.
 Find out more
Who’s it for: School pupils and their parents
Barrier-free access to the building and premises is available.
The service is free.
Please make an appointment.
